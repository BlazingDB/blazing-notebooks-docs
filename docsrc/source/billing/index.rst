=======
Billing
=======

BlazingSQL Notebooks offers you a simple and friendly interface to buy 
credits so you can start working on private clusters with ease. 

Credits
=======

Credits are the *currency* of Blazing Notebooks. By purchasing credits
you gain access to the easiest way to launch GPU clusters with up to 
64 GPUs (see :doc:`private clusters <../private_clusters/cluster/index>`).

Available credits
-----------------

On the top of the BlazingSQL Notebooks app page, 
a current total number of remaining credits is always visible.
As long as you have some credits available you can 
create and use private clusters
on Blazing Notebooks.

Purchasing credits
------------------

1. Select how many credits you want to buy.

        </div>

|

2. Enter you credit card information and click on **Pay** button.

    <div class="col-lg-12 col-md-12 col-sm-12 col-xs-12 d-flex text-center image">

to track your spending.
